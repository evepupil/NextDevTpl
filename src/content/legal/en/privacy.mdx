# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

NextjsTpl ("NextjsTpl", "we", "us", or "our") operates the NextjsTpl service (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Service, and the choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account and Profile Information

When you create or update an account, we may collect:

- **Email address**
- **Name and profile details** you choose to provide
- **Authentication data** from OAuth providers (e.g., Google, GitHub)

### 1.2 Content and Inputs

We collect the content you submit to the Service, including:

- **Text, files, or URLs** you upload or paste to generate flashcards
- **Generated flashcards and exports** (e.g., Anki packages)
- **Metadata** such as file names, sizes, and upload timestamps

### 1.3 Usage and Device Data

We may collect usage information such as:

- IP address, browser type, device identifiers
- Pages viewed, actions taken, and time spent
- Diagnostic logs and performance data

### 1.4 Payment Information

If you purchase a subscription or credits, payments are processed by Creem. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers. Creem may collect billing details as part of payment processing.

### 1.5 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Service and remember your preferences. See our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookie-policy) for details.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide and operate the Service**, including AI-powered flashcard generation
- **Create and manage accounts**, authentication, and user support
- **Process payments** and manage subscriptions or credits
- **Improve and maintain** product performance and reliability
- **Communicate with you** about updates, security, and offers (you can opt out of marketing emails)
- **Detect and prevent abuse**, fraud, or security issues

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK)

If you are in the EEA or UK, we rely on the following legal bases:

- **Contract** - to provide the Service
- **Legitimate interests** - to improve, secure, and operate the Service
- **Consent** - for optional features such as marketing or analytics cookies
- **Legal obligations** - to comply with applicable laws

## 4. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the Service and meet legal obligations. You can delete content or close your account, after which we will delete or de-identify your data within a reasonable period, unless retention is required by law.

## 5. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries where we or our service providers operate, which may have different data protection laws than your country. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

## 6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS) and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
