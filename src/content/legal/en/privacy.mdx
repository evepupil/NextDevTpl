While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This may include:

- **Email address** - Used for account creation, communication, and support

- **Payment Information** - Processed securely through Stripe; we do not store full credit card numbers

### 1.2 Usage Data

We may also collect information on how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include:

- Your computer's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
- Browser type and version

- The time spent on those pages
- Unique device identifiers
- Other diagnostic data

### 1.3 Tracking & Cookies Data

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. See our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookie-policy) for more details.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

- **To provide and maintain the Service** - Including to monitor the usage of our Service
- **To notify you about changes** - To notify you about changes to our Service
- **To provide customer support** - To respond to inquiries and provide assistance
- **To gather analysis** - To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our Service
- **To monitor usage** - To monitor the usage of our Service and detect technical issues
- **To prevent fraud** - To detect, prevent and address technical issues and fraudulent activity
- **To send communications** - To provide you with news, special offers, and general information about other goods, services, and events (you can opt out at any time)

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are from the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for collecting and using the personal information described in this Privacy Policy depends on the Personal Data we collect and the specific context:

- **Performance of a contract** - Processing your data is necessary for the performance of a contract with you
- **Legitimate interests** - Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests
- **Consent** - Where you have given us consent to process your personal data
- **Legal obligation** - We need to process your data to comply with a legal obligation

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy:

If you are located outside the United States and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data to the United States and process it there.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.

## 6. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your Personal Data:

- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Access controls and authentication measures
- Secure payment processing through Stripe

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

### 7.2 For EEA Residents (GDPR)

- **Restrict Processing** - Request restriction of processing your data
- **Object to Processing** - Object to processing of your data
